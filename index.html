01 · About

Who am I?

I'm a dual-degree engineer, holding a BEng in Bioengineering from McGill, and currently completing my MASc in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Toronto.

My graduate research focuses on hydrogen combustion for aviation gas turbines, simulating premixed H₂ flames using LES/RANS coupled with tabulated chemistry (FPI). I'm implementing a C++/Python correction within a Navier-Stokes PCM-FPI solver to capture preferential diffusion, a critical physical effect in lean hydrogen combustion.

Outside the lab I do triathlon, and will be competing at the World Triathlon Championship this fall (Pontevedra 2026, Olympic distance). When I’m not working, you’ll often find me outdoors, whether backcountry skiing in the winter or kitesurfing in the summer.
